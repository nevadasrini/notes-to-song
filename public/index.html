NOTES TO SONG.



ARE YOU STRESSED?
Do you have a major test tomorrow? Do you have a difficulty remembering a lot of information in a short amount of time? Or do you feel demotivated while studying and just need a way to make the process of reviewing information more fun and exciting? Either way, we have just what you need. Song Converter takes your notes and turns them into catchy melodies according to your specifications. You can even take a look at the notes that other people submitted to the site.

It's easy!


STEP 1.

Sign up for an account.






STEP 2.

Type your notes into the converter, set your preferences, and submit.

STEP 3.

STEP 4.

Click on a song to take a look at it.
